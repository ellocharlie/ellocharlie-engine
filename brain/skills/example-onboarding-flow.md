# Customer Onboarding Flow

When a new customer starts their free trial:

1. CX Lead agent sends welcome message within 15 minutes
2. Schedule onboarding call with Nicholas (first 10 customers: always; after: if Plus tier)
3. Create onboarding project in ellocharlie with default checklist
4. Set up health monitoring with 48-hour first-check
5. Add to weekly check-in rotation for first 90 days

## Context Assembly
- Pull deal information from CRM (how they found us, what tier, team size)
- Check if they came from a referral (different welcome flow)
- Review any pre-sale support interactions

## Success Criteria
- Customer reaches "first value" within 24 hours
- All checklist items completed within 7 days
- NPS survey sent at day 14
